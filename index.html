Near places to visit from DBATU

Shrivardhan
In Shrivardhan Beach Visitors can enjoy water sports like swimming and boating, and the area is also known for its historical significance. Shrivardhan Beach is located near other popular beaches like Harihareshwar and Diveagar.
 
Mahabaleshwar
Mahabaleshwar is a picturesque hill station located in Maharashtra, India. It is renowned for its natural beauty, including lush green hills, stunning viewpoints, and strawberry farms.
 
Della resort
Experience high octane rides, award winning restaurants and more at Della Adventure! #1 Rated Park in Lonavla. Multi Cuisine Restaurants.
 
Karjat
Karjat is a scenic town located in Maharashtra, India, known for its natural beauty, adventure activities, and serene ambiance. Situated amidst the Western Ghats, it offers lush green landscapes, waterfalls, and picturesque hills. Karjat is popular for trekking, hiking.
 
Mahabaleshwar
The Mahabaleshwar town offers a pleasant climate and serves as a popular retreat for tourists seeking a refreshing escape from the city. Mahabaleshwar is known for its panoramic views, waterfalls, and serene atmosphere. It is an ideal destination for nature lovers.

Harihareshwar
Harihareshwar is a coastal town in Maharashtra, India, known for its serene beach and temples. The town is home to the Harihareshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and the Kalbhairav Temple. It is a popular pilgrimage destination attracting devotees during festivals like Mahashivratri.
 
Diveagar
The Diveagar beach is known for its delicious seafood dishes, and nearby attractions include Harihareshwar Beach, Shrivardhan Beach, Murud-Janjira Fort, and the Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary. Diveagar Beach provides a tranquil and relaxing atmosphere 
Raigad
Raigad Fort is situated on a hilltop in the Sahyadri Mountain range, approximately 820 meters (2,700 feet) above sea level. It is located around 25 kilometers (15.5 miles) north of Mahad and 130 kilometers (80.7 miles) south of Mumbai. 
Imagicca
Imagicaa is a themed entertainment destination featuring world class rides & attractions. Plan your visit to this perfect family holiday destination near Mumbai, Pune & Lonavala. Holi party. 
Devkund
Devkund Waterfall is located in Bhira Patnus it has become extremely crowded and dangerous place. It is the confluence of three waterfalls and is said to be the origin of Kundalika River. 
Panchgani
Panchgani was developed by the British during the British Raj as a summer resort under the supervision of Lord John Chesson in the 1860s. Panchgani was developed as a retirement place because it remained pleasant throughout the year 
Pali Ganpati
Pali Ganpati Temple is originally built in the 11th century. Moreshvar Vitthal Sindkar contributed to the construction of the temple in 1640. Also, it was originally made of wood and was renovated in the year 1760 by Moropant Dada Phadnavis
